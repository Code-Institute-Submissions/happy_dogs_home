About Us

Happy Dogs Home is a registered charity based in Shilton

We rescue, rehabilitate and re-home dogs to give them the better future they deserve

All our animals are health checked, inoculated & identi-chipped and leave with 4 weeks free pet insurance

To find out how to adopt and book in a visit please fill out our Contact Form at the bottom of our site
